The importance of conditioning

Increase Stamina

Increasing endurance will allow you to train for longer. This will mean less recovery time in lessons and routines.

Increase Strength & Flexibility

Bodyweight resistance training not only helps you toward your goals, it also reduces the risk of injury.

Relieve Stress

Exercise and training for disciplines you love release endorphins in your body, making you feel happy.
