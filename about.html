About us

The Company

Aerial Sport and Fitness has been successfully training and coaching aerial athletes of all abilities since 2014.

Lessons are available to book.

The Coach: Carla

"My journey started in gymnastics, where I competed internationally for Great Britain. I then trained as a professional dancer, and travelled the world with this profession. My love of Aerial Sports was born as a combination of the two. I qualified instruct aerial disciplines and opened the company in 2014. It's wonderful to watch the mental and physical growth and evolution of my clients".
